the red dead redemption pasta

do you want to make this amazing pasta to impress every one? well you can follow this simple recipe and kill everyones expectations. This pasta will impress every one and thats a gaurantee welp lets dive into this recipe

ingredents(serves 2)

  • pasta(penne) 200gms
  • tomatoes 2large
  • cheese 50gms
  • cheese spread 50gms
  • garlic choped 5-6 pods
  • onions choped 3 large onions
  • capsicum red green and yellow each 1 bowl
  • paneer(cotage cheese) choped 250 gms
  • spring onion 2 bowls
  • oregano to taste
  • salt to taste
  • chilli flakes to taste
  • peper to taste
  • thyme to taste
  • basil to taste
  • water to make it wet
  • oil 6 table spoons

steps to make the red dead redemption pasta


  1. take the pasta and boil it for around 15 min
  2. take the tomatoes and boil them after boiling peel them and put them in a pan
  3. add the onions, garlic, cheese spread , and a little water as well as peper, thyme, basil, chilli flakes, salt, oregano
  4. now let the sauce heat up on the simmer for 10 minutes
  5. now add the sauce to the pasta
  6. add all the vegitables to the pasta and sauce
  7. grate some cheese on top of the pasta and bake the pasta
  8. season the pasta with oregano, thyme, chilli flakes and salt
  9. serve the pasta hot , and your red dead redemption pasta is ready for eating
